Theorem 4.9.

Let f:X→Yf:X\rightarrow Y be a quotient map, and let ZZ be a top. sp. Let h:X→Zh:X\rightarrow Z be a function such that h⁢(x)=h⁢(x′)h(x)=h(x^{\prime}) whenever f⁢(x)=f⁢(x′)f(x)=f(x^{\prime}). These are not hard to prove, but they are results to keep in mind.
